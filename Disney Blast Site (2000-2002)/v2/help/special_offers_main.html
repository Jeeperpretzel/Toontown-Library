· What is Disney's Blast Super Mega Prize Center?
It's a place inside Disney's Blast full of collectible toys and games. It was designed with your child in mind, to maximize his/her Disney's Blast experience. To play, members simply select games, win coins, and use them to buy the digital toys of their choice.

· What are digital toys?
Digital toys are electronic gadgets that can be played within Disney's Blast. They include Disney character trading cards, action games, animated puzzles, robot kits, magical music boxes, and much more!

· How does my child get digital toys?
To get digital toys, members need to win coins. Winning coins is easy-just come to Disney's Blast and play games.

· Coins are awarded to each Disney's Blast member for:
· Logging on to Disney's Blast: 100 coins
· Playing a game or activity shown featured on the Super Mega Prize Center Prize Center monitor: 100 coins or 200 coins

· How does my child trade coins for digital toys?
From the main Disney's Blast home page, click on the Super Mega Prize Center. Inside there are three separate areas:

1) The lobby where Donald Duck greets you.
2) The Shop where children can see the available digital toys and exchange coins for various items. To select an item, simply click on the item or on the "buy" button.

· How does my child play with a digital toy?
Members can access any of the digital toys inside the Disney's Blast Personal Digital Assistant (PDA). The PDA is locating in the Super Mega Prize Center lobby, and is also available in the store. In addition to your user name, the PDA highlights the number of points currently available.

· How do I know what rank my child has achieved?
A child's member rank appears on their PDA, and it will update as the child accumulates more points and advances ranks.

· Is it possible for my child to lose his/her ranking or coins?
Disney's Blast will never reduce a ranking level or deduct coins from any member. These can only be lost due to the following conditions:

· Exchange of coins for digital toys
· Cancellation of membership for the entire account
· Cancellation of a child's account in Disney's Blast Member Services

· What happens if I cancel my child's account in Member Services?
If you cancel any child account, ALL information for that child is deleted - this includes all points, coins, and digital prizes. Unfortunately, this information cannot be restored.

· If I cancel my Disney's Blast membership and decide to come back, is it possible to restore my child's ranking, coins, and digital toy collection?
If an account is canceled, ALL information for that account is deleted - this includes all points, coins, and digital prizes. Unfortunately, this information cannot be restored.

· What happens if coins, digital toys, or a ranking disappear in error?
If any member believes an item was lost in error, please contact us and provide detailed information about which items were lost, including an explanation of how and when the error was noticed.

· What if my coins or digital prizes disappear?
Because of the way the Internet works, sometimes Disney’s Blast might not be able to form a full connection to the Super Mega Prize Center database. If this happens, it will seem as if the digital prizes or the total number of coins that you have “blank out.” Actually, your digital prizes and your coins are still safe in the Super Mega Prize Center, but are temporarily not visible. In the meantime, continue to enjoy Disney's Blast and rest assured that your coins will reappear once the technical issues are cleared up.
