Here's everything your computer needs to fully enjoy Disney's Blast:
 
For PC users we recommend:
Operating system: Windows 98 or better
Processor: Pentium II 300MHz or better
Browser: IE 5.0, Netscape 4.74, AOL 5.0 or higher
Plug-ins: Flash 6, Shockwave 8
Monitor: 800x600 pixels or larger
RAM: 64MB or better
  


For MAC users we recommend:
Operating system: 9.04 or better
Processor: G3 or better
Browser: IE 5.0, Netscape 4.74 or higher
Plug-ins: Flash 6, Shockwave 8
Monitor: 800x600 pixels or larger
RAM: 64MB or better

NOTE: AOL browsers for the MAC is not supported.

If you do not have the proper plugins to use Disney's Blast, please go to the Software Center under the Parents menu within Blast.
