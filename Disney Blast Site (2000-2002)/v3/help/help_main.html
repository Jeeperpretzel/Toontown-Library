Member Services / Account Information

How do I update my Member Information?

To update your information, just click on "Parents' Corner" from the home page, and then go to "Help Center." Within Help Center, click on "Account Information and Safety Settings". From there, you'll need to log in to change your information. Please note, only the Parent Account holder can modify account information.

Will my Membership information be posted anywhere online?

We will never post any personal information online including e-mail address. When playing some of the games within Disney's Blast, you may post your Member Name in Top Scores of the Games area. If you win a sweepstakes or contest, we may post your first name, the first initial of your last name and your age, city and state. For more info, check out our Privacy Policy located in the footer section of the Disney.com homepage.

How do I add or remove a family member?

Simply select "Parents' Corner" from the home page of Disney's Blast, and then go to "Help Center." From there, select "Account Information and Safety Settings." Add or remove a family member is one of the options presented in this section. With Disney's Blast, you can add up to 4 additional family members to your main account. Don't forget, you'll need to set the parental controls for each family member added.

What are Parental Controls and why do I need them?

Parental Controls provide you with the most flexibility in designing your child's experience on Disney's Blast. Here you can decide if you want your child to participate in multi-player games, communicate with others on Disney's Blast with restricted chat (not open chat) or send electronic cards to others on the Internet. As some new features are added on Disney's Blast, you'll have the option to include/exclude them in your child's experience. Please note, if you have not updated your Parental Controls, don't worry - the default setting provides the strictest controls allowed within Disney's Blast.

Technical Support / Log-in

Why do I need Shockwave plug-ins?

The Shockwave plug-ins are used to enhance your experience at Disney's Blast. Some of the games or activities within Disney's Blast require the plug-ins to be downloaded first in order to start the program. And without these programs we could not bring you all the fun and excitement you expect from Disney.

Where can I download the Shockwave plug-ins?

When you reach a game or activity that requires the Shockwave plug-in, you will be prompted to download the plug-in at that time. You can also download the plug-in in advance at the software center for Disney's Blast. You can find the software center within Parent's Corner and/or at the Disney's Blast log-in page.

Why can't I download the Shockwave plug-ins?

One reason for this problem might be the browser you are using.

For America Online Users:
Please check which version of AOL you have. If you have a 3.x or lower version of AOL, this might be why you are having difficulty downloading these programs. Contact America
Online for the new version of its software.

For Internet Explorer Users:
Please check the version of Internet Explorer that you currently have. If the version of IE is 3.x or lower, please go to the Microsoft Web site at www.microsoft.com* to get the latest version.

*Please note, due to our safety features, you will have to log out of Disney's Blast to link to the Microsoft Web site.

For Netscape Users:
Please check the version of Netscape Navigator that you currently have. If you have version 3.x or lower, you will need to upgrade your browser with the most current version. You can get the latest version of Netscape Navigator by going to www.netscape.com.*

*Please note, due to our safety features, you will have to log out of Disney's Blast to link to the Netscape Web site.

Blast Parents' Corner

What is Parents' Corner?

The Disney's Blast Parent's Corner is an area within Disney's Blast designed just for Parents. It provides information on the color-coding used to highlight the age-appropriateness of the games, stories and activities. It provides information on the various learning categories and a Fun Finder to allow you to quickly search for the most appropriate content for your child based on age, learning category or their favorite Disney character. The Parents' Corner is also the area within Disney's Blast to update and or change your individual account information.

Where do I find the Parents' Corner?

You can get to the Parents' Corner at anytime within Disney's Blast by either clicking on the wooden sign on the bottom right hand corner in the Disney's Blast home page, which says "Parents' Corner", clicking on the Parents' Corner icon on the upper right hand corner of Blast Dome home page, or by clicking on the Parents' Corner icon on the lower right hand corner of the Mouse House Jr. home page.

What does color coding mean?

Color coding is an easy way to locate the games, activities and stories that are right for your child. Each color represents a different age group-yellow for ages 3-6, blue for ages 6-8 and red for 8-10.

What does educational category mean?

All the content in Disney's Blast was sorted into general categories, each of which describes the particular learning skill being promoted. Learning skills such as Brainbusters, Listening, Word Workshop, Mathmagic, and Creativity Corner, will help you choose the most appropriate activity for your child. Check out the Fun Finder for more information on how to use these categories.

What is the Fun Finder and how can it help me?

Fun Finder allows you to discover new Disney's Blast games and activities by age, category and character name. Fun Finder is located in the Blast Parents' Corner. Simply select the child's age, the educational category or favorite Disney character and click on "Find" and the first 10 items that fit your criteria will be listed on the upper right hand side of the Find Finder. You can access any one of the items listed by simply clicking on the item you want.

Password Information and Member Name

How do I change or retrieve my password?

To change your password for Disney's Blast, simply click on "Parents' Corner" anywhere within Disney's Blast, and then go to "Account Information and Safety Settings" and you can change your password under "Modify Account Information" Please note, only the Parent Account holder can modify account information. If you forgot your password or Member Name, you can click on the appropriate button on the top of the Log-in page and follow the instructions to retrieve the information you need.

Can I change my Member Name?

You can change your password or any of the child account information at any time. Unfortunately, the Primary Member Name on the account cannot be changed.

E-mail Preferences

What kind of mailings should I expect to receive if I opt in to the mailing list?

You'll receive e-mails telling you about new and exciting happenings at Disney's Blast plus other information you requested. Examples include Disney's Blast Gazettes - bi-weekly newsletters highlighting the new content added that week inside Disney's Blast, regular newsletters from Disney, and/or Birthday E-cards from Mickey and his friends sent the month your child was born.

Why should I keep my e-mail address current on my account?

By keeping your e-mail address updated with us, you make it possible for us to contact you if you need help with your password, provide important information about your account and send you updates on all the new and exciting features coming to Disney's Blast.

What if I don't want to receive any additional e-mails?

If you would like to unsubscribe to any of our e-mails, please do NOT reply to the e-mail. The quickest and best way to unsubscribe to any unwanted mail is to log in to our E-mail Preference Center, and make your selections there. The E-Mail Preference Center is located in "Parents' Corner", under "Account Information & Safety Settings". Once you log in, you can link to the E-mail Preference Center and change your selections.

How do I change my e-mail address?

If you would like e-mails going to a different e-mail address then the one you signed up with, just log into the "Parents' Corner", under "Account Information & Safety Settings". Once you are logged in, you can modify your e-mail under Member Information.

Credit Card Safety, Payment methods and Billing Questions

How do I update my credit card information?

For your protection, no one but the Parent account holder can access credit card information online. You can update your credit card information and view your billing history by logging into the "Parents' Corner" from the home page, then going to "Account Information & Safety Settings". Once you log in, you can modify your information under "Address and Credit Card".

How and when will Disney's Blast charges appear?

Subscription charges will appear as www.disneyblast.com on your credit card after your free trial period, if applicable. If you are a monthly subscriber, charges will automatically appear on your credit card statement each month. If you are an annual subscriber, you will be charge in full for the year, then on your anniversary date thereafter. If you are a quarterly subscriber, charges will automatically appear on your credit card statement every three months.

Disney Adventures Magazine

How do I become eligible to receive the Disney Adventures Magazine?

You must be an annual subscriber and reside in the United States to receive the magazine. It will be mailed out within six to 8 weeks of your subscription date. The magazine will be sent to the name and address of the Parent Account holder.

How many issues should I expect?

Disney Adventures Magazine has 10 issues per year, and will take approximately 6-8 weeks for the first delivery.

What if I didn't receive my magazine this month?

Contact Customer Service at (503) 626-5474 or toll-free: (877) 492-6846, between the hours of 9 a.m. to 10 p.m. Eastern Standard Time Monday through Friday and let us know you did not receive a copy. We will make sure you receive the next issue promptly.
